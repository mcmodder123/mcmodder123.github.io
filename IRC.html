Inhibition Release Candle


Scented with tones of ylang ylang, clary sage and orange essential oils, this candle helps and promotes inner peace and emotional balance. Its unique essential oil blend was carefully prepared to also improve nervousness, scarcity, postpartum depression, anxiety and aids self expression of repressed feelings.


Burning Instructions

When you first light a candle, let it burn until the wax pools at the edges of the vessel or tin. That will ensure that the wax has reached its maximum melting point and will avoid creating too much heat. Next time you light the candle, make sure to trim the wick to 1/4 inch before you light it. This will prevent a large flame from forming and ensure that your candle lasts longer.


Keep Candle free of any foreign materials including matches and wick trimmings.


Only burn the candle on a level, fire resistant surface.
